Punjab Announces 12th Class Second Annual Exam Schedule 2025

By Faiq Sindhu Sep 19, 2025

The Punjab Boards Committee of Chairpersons (PBCC), in its meeting held on 13 August 2025, finalized the schedule for the declaration of results of the Higher Secondary School Certificate (HSSC) First Annual Examinations 2025 and the commencement of the Second Annual Examinations 2025. The notification was issued on August 28, 2025, by PBCC/BISE Lahore.

The notification was released under the supervision of the Controller of Examinations, PBCC / BISE Lahore. Contact details of the Secretary, PBCC, and Liaison Officer were also provided in the notice for further queries.

PBCC has emphasized that all students and institutions must strictly adhere to the given deadlines for submitting the admission form. The schedule provides no mention of extensions beyond the final dates.
